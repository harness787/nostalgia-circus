ABOUT US

We are a jazz/indie duo based out of Conway, AR. We play live gigs and have and have a core belief that you can make good music with what you have access to. For this reason, we record virtually all our music on our phones. We plan to put more and more content out over the next year and hope you join us on our journey. Welcome to the Nostalgia Circus.
